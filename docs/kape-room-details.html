Room Description

Our spacious 30 m² (323 ft²) Superior Rooms offer luxury and comfort. Ideal for leisure and business travellers, our Superior Rooms feature large windows with panoramic views of the city, a plush, comfortable king bed, an executive working desk, a luxurious bathtub with a separate shower and a complimentary mini bar for your complete comfort and convenience during your stay with us.

Experience the epitome of Nairobi hospitality with our attentive service and world-class amenities designed to make your stay unforgettable.
